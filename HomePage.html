Our Mission: Informing current and new residents on our trails and to ultimatly encourage people to hike!

In the Pacific Northwest, there's ostensibly endless options of trails, peaks and mountains to explore. Here on our site, we will provide 5 different trails in the Pacfic Northwest that we will reccomend to you. Each trail will include the location of the trail, distance of the hike, elevation and lastly the rating of each of the hikes.

We suggest everyone comes prepared! You should bring a backpack, a map, water, food, flashlight, cellphone, change of socks, rain jacket, rope, knife and a first aid kit.
